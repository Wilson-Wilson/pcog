The vision of the Church of God in Jamaica is focused on the realization of that day, when the organization will, by the grace of God, attain the level of Christian leadership and stewardship, which will impact significantly on the healing of the nation.

Under the guidance of the Blessed Holy Spirit, the Mission of the Church of God in Jamaica is to win souls for the Kingdom of God through holistic interactions consistent with the mandate of the Great commission GIVEN TO THE Church by our Lord Jesus Christ, in St Mathew 28: 19-20.
